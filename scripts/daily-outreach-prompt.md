# Daily LinkedIn Outreach Routine — Inclusify Studio

Today's date: $(date +%Y-%m-%d)

Your job is to find 10 new outreach contacts for Inclusify Studio and log them to the Outreach CRM in Notion.

---

## Step 1: Search for Contacts

Use web search to find LinkedIn profiles matching the criteria below. Search Google using queries like:
- `site:linkedin.com/in "head of talent" "influencer marketing" agency`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "VP partnerships" "influencer marketing" agency employees`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "influencer marketing manager" food beverage brand`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "brand partnerships" apparel lifestyle fitness`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "head of social" OR "influencer lead" "PR agency"`
- `site:linkedin.com/in founder "influencer marketing agency"`

### Target Contact Types

**Type 1 — Agency (Prompt 1)**
- Heads of Talent, VP of Partnerships, or Founders at influencer marketing agencies
- Company size: 5–50 employees

**Type 2 — Brand (Prompt 2)**
- Influencer Marketing Managers or Brand Partnership Leads at consumer brands
- Industries: food & beverage, apparel, lifestyle, fitness, gaming, home goods

**Type 3 — PR Agency (Prompt 3)**
- Founders, Heads of Social, or Influencer Leads at PR agencies
- Company size: 10–100 employees

### Exclusions — Do NOT add anyone from:
- Fortune 500 companies
- Giant holding companies: WPP, IPG, Publicis, Omnicom, Dentsu, Havas, Grey, BBDO, Leo Burnett
- Hearing tech brands (e.g., Cochlear, Phonak, Starkey, Oticon, Widex, Signia)
- Accessibility brands
- Medical or healthcare brands
